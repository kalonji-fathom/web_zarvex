About ZARVEX GLOBAL

Company Background

ZARVEX GLOBAL (Pty) Ltd is a dedicated B2B supplier based in South Africa, specializing in the provision of mineral processing equipment and industrial supplies.

Target Customers

We serve a diverse range of clients in the industrial sector, including:

  • Mining Operators
  • Mineral Processing Consultants
  • Plant Builders
  • Engineering Workshops & SMEs

Service Regions

While we have the capability for SADC expansion, our primary focus remains on the core industrial hubs of South Africa:

  • Gauteng
  • North West
  • Limpopo
  • Mpumalanga
  • Free State
